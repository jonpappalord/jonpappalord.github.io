## About

Luca Pappalardo is a researcher in the Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining group at ISTI-CNR. His work focuses on understanding human behavior through data, with applications spanning mobility, social systems, cities, and AI. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Research interests

- Human mobility and urban dynamics
- Data science and machine learning
- Artificial intelligence and society
- Complex networks and socio-economic systems

Recent SNS materials and events also place his work within the FAIR ecosystem and broader discussions on the societal impact of AI. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
