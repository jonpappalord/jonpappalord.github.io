Luca Pappalardo is a Senior Researcher at the Institute of Information Science and Technologies of the Italian National Research Council (ISTI-CNR), in the Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining laboratory.

His research sits at the crossroads of data science, artificial intelligence, human mobility, urban systems, and computational social science.

He is also associated with initiatives at Scuola Normale Superiore, including FAIR-related research activities and public academic events on AI and digital platforms.
